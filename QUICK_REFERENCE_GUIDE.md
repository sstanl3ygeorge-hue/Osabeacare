## 🎓 Key Concepts

### Destination Register
Mapping of template types → system locations. Example:
- `su_care_plans` = Care Plans tab in ServiceUserProfilePage
- `incidents` = Incident Management module
- `recruitment` = HR recruitment workflow

### Category
Archive grouping:
- `service-user-care-plans` (36 templates)
- `operational-documents` (95+ templates)
- `hr-documents` (120+ templates)
- `policies-compliance` (90+ templates)
- `quality-compliance-records` (80+ templates)

### Section Type
Logical grouping within categories:
- `service_user_tab` (care plan tabs)
- `operational` (incident, body map, etc.)
- `hr` (recruitment, disciplinary, etc.)
- `policy` (safeguarding, H&S, etc.)
- `audit` (audit records, competency, etc.)
